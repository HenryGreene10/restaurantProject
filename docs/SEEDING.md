# Seed Data Requirement (Mandatory)

Before moving past the first vertical slice, provide a realistic seed script that creates:
- 2+ restaurants with distinct brand configs and custom domains/subdomains.
- Full menus per restaurant: categories, items, variants, and modifier groups/options (min/max/required).
- 30+ fake customers per restaurant (phones), with SMS opt‑in flags.
- 200+ historical orders per restaurant across statuses and times to power analytics and the assistant.
- Push subscriptions where feasible for demo (plus safe fallbacks).

Seeds must be idempotent and clearly tagged as demo data.
